Hello, I am Yian Yu, a PhD candidate in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the Unversity of Science and Technology. I am privileged to be under the guidance of Prof. Jian Qing Shi. Research Interests: My research interests integrates statistical modeling with theoretical foundations, machine learning, and data-driven insights to transform complex data into actionable solutions for real-world challenges. My past and ongoning projects encompasses the following domains:

  • Multimodal large-scale spatio-temporal data modeling with efficient Bayesian computational algorithms (Begin in Aug. 2025).

  • Irregular functional data analysis with applications (Sept. 2020-Present).

  • Comparison of Treatments with ordinal response in clinical trials (Jan. 2020-July 2022).

  • Questionarie verification (Mar. 2020-Aug. 2023).

Research Overview: My Ph.D. focus on advancing FDA for irregular (data with shape constraints, or varying covariance structures), high-dimensional datasets, emphasizing applications in gait data acquired from individ uals with neurodegenerative diseases via wearable devices. During this period, I conducted an efficient clinical trail design for compariosons of treatments with ordinal response with sequential monitoring and response-adaptive randomization under the supervisor Prof. Siu Hung Cheung . Co-developed a novel questionare with Department of Neurology, Shenzhen People's Hospital, I conducted the structual data analysis and statistically validated it for somatic symptom disorder screening.
